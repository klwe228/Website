About Us

Sweet Weeks Bakery was founded in 2016 by Danielle and Katherine Weeks. They decided to combine their love of food and their love of family together to create a bakery that not only fulfills your sweet tooth, but provides a comfortable place to relax. They seek to provide a place for food, whole-hearted laughter, and relaxation in a family atmosphere.

Currently there are three locations - Hudson, WI (the girl's hometown), Lexington, KY (Katherine's Alumnus), and Denver, CO (Danielle's Alumnus). They hope to expand and create locations across the US for everyone to enjoy.

Please feel free to check out the rest of our website and click on the "Contact Us" page to give us your thoughts/pay us a visit.

Come for the pastries; stay for the atmosphere.

Sweet Weeks motto
